# VidhyanAI

**Your personal AI teacher that actually shows up every day.**

[vidhyanai.com](https://vidhyanai.com)

## What is VidhyanAI?

Think of it as having a private tutor who never gets tired, never cancels, and always knows exactly where you left off.

You tell it what you want to learn — say, web development, data science, digital marketing, or anything else — and it figures out the rest. It builds a study plan for you, writes your lessons from scratch each day, and delivers them at whatever time you prefer. If something doesn't click, you ask a question and it explains it right there, in context, without making you feel silly for asking.

No textbooks. No pre-recorded videos. No generic content copy-pasted from the internet. Every lesson is written specifically for you, based on what you already know and what you've covered so far.

---

## Why does this exist?

Most online learning platforms give everyone the same content in the same order. That works for some people, but not most.

VidhyanAI was built around a simple idea: learning should adapt to the person, not the other way around. The platform tracks your progress, remembers what you've done, and adjusts what comes next. It also runs on a schedule, so you don't have to remember to open an app — it sends you a reminder when your session is ready.

---

## How does it actually work?

Here's the honest, no-jargon version:

**1. You sign up and tell it what you want to learn.**
Takes about two minutes. You pick your topic and set a time each day when you want to study.

**2. It builds your personal study roadmap.**
The system maps out all the topics you'll need to cover, in an order that actually makes sense — foundations first, advanced stuff later.

**3. Every day, it writes your lesson.**
Not pulls from a library. Writes. A fresh lesson, tailored to where you are in your roadmap, with real sources attached so you can dig deeper if you want.

**4. You read it, learn, and ask questions.**
There's a built-in question box right on the lesson page. Ask anything. The system understands your current lesson and gives you a relevant answer, not a generic one.

**5. It tracks your progress automatically.**
You can see exactly how far you've come, which topics are done, and what's coming up next — all in a clean dashboard.

---

## What happens behind the scenes?

Without going into technical details: VidhyanAI runs several specialized AI programs that each handle a specific job — one builds your study plan, one writes your lessons, one answers your questions, one sends your reminders, and so on. They all work together automatically.

The lessons are generated using one of the most capable AI models available today (Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash), combined with a system that searches for relevant, accurate information before writing anything. That's what keeps the content reliable and grounded rather than just made up.
